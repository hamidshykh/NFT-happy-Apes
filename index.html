About Us

What are the Happy Apes?

The Happy Apes are a collection 1,500 Apes ripped straight from the 60s! They have brought their entire personas, cardigans and all. However, they are simply happy, there is no saying that there is nothing more sinister underneath the hood.

HAPPY APES GAME

The team has discussed the actual premise for the game, however, we have not gone in-depth on the mechanics and game itself, it has just been a light overview. In this message, we aim to summarize the game and answer all questions pertaining to it.

*What is the lore behind the game?*

One may wonder, what has lead these absolutely adorable apes to go savage and rip each other apart? Well it is quite simple, the fateful day that Dr. Albert Hofmann took an accidental trip, something much more sinister happened on Earth ###...

Instead of using the substance to explore for new cures to respiratory illnesses, the government had sent Dr. Hofmann to harness its powerful capabilities for new, clean energy that could be generated by the power of the mind. The experiments took place on various primates, however, the primate Pan Paniscus had an extreme reaction. This reaction is what the scientist THOUGHT would lead him to a groundbreaking discover...

After further experimenting, it became clear the forces they toyed with were beyond their understanding. However, they were carried away foolishly in their attempts to play God. The apes developed powers after this period of experimentation which manifested as dimensional energy reminiscent of a wormhole.

This power became too much for anyone to harvest, but the scientists were too deep into their work to stop. Dr. Hofmann had been driven to the brink of madness seemingly by his work, but he was unknowingly being driven mad by the substance that made him the well renound scientist was.

Eventually, the apes were able to escape and ventured across points in space and time with their new found powers, leading them to being in the Happy Apes video game!

*What will the gameplay be like?*

The gameplay will be similar to the popular game "Worms"! Worms is an artillery tactical video game which has small platoons of anthropomorphic characters (worms) battling each other across multiple platform/deformable landscapes. The objective of this game is to eliminate the opposing teams and be a member of the sole surviving team. It is important to note that the worms series featured turn based combat on a 2D battlefield, so the games tend to be strategy-based.

*What will the style of the game be?*

The game will have the style of pixel art. However, we will not allow the game to falter in quality! The team will hold ourselves to the standard we did with the NFT art and the game will be just as stunning itself!

*Will my NFT have any use in the game?*

Yes! Your NFT will be able to offer special rewards like a token multiplier once it has been staked and mutated into an unhinged ape! The 1/1 NFTs will also offer special effects in game like awesome weapons, multipliers, and more!

*How will the staking feature work?*

There will be a special mode that allows you to wager a certain amount of $LSD against the opponent and have your apes battle to the death for the prize! The house will take a small cut of a few % off of the total winnings as a tax, however the rest will be rewarded to the victor! This mode will incentivize a skill based wager and we may eventually expand to wagers of other things... (Allow your imagination to run wild!)

*When will we see demos/teasers for the game?*

Although nothing is set in stone, the team hopes that demos can be released within a couple weeks of launch. The team has hired both a developer and an artist, however the only thing that is missing is the funding itself. Our current artist will be making art and teasers as soon as he has the time available so within a couple weeks we will see various posts go out containing said art!

ROADMAP

Full Launch (Late January 2022)


The first phase of the Happy Apes roadmap will include the marketing, mint, and secondary listing. The Happy Apes team has acknowledged that these are the barebone essentials of a roadmap, so we will not labor over them as it should already be established our team does the bare minimum. We do not mess around.

Establishing the DAO (Late January 2022)


The team will create a place where verified holders of the Happy Apes NFT can conglomerate and make connections! This will take place shortly after mint and it will allow the community to flourish!

Community Push (Late January/ Early February 2022)


In an effort to further build our community in anticipation of the events to come, the team will come together to strengthen the community via a range of events. This could go from simple raids to large scale competitions for prizes!

$LSD/Game development (February 2022)


At this time, we will begin the distribution of our token $LSD! Additionally, we will see more demos/updates on the Happy Apes PVP game with a twist! Although it is not stated before, we will have already begun game production long before this and the production will start within days of launch of discord. It is important to mention both of these seemingly foreign concepts together as they will be tied together in terms of utility. Each of these will be funded with large amounts of liquidity.

STAKING (Late February 2022)


Among the first utilities of $LSD, staking will be performed by sending your Happy Ape to the "Psych Ward". In this staking system, there will be tiers and each one unhinging your ape ever so slightly more.

LSD your NFT (Late February/ Early March 2022)


Redeeming a large amount of original Happy Apes NFTs or a lesser number of "mutated" Apes will allow you to have your NFT mutate its traits. These mutations will reward higher yields in staking and token multipliers!

Full Game Release (Early March 2022)


As time continues, the team will push further information about what exactly the HA (Happy Apes) PVP game will contain. This game will have utility that ties in the token $LSD and be one of a kind for the SOL ecosystem. The team aims to launch the full game within a month, releasing demos and teasers along the way.

Future development (March 2022)


After the creation and perfection of the original PVP game, the team aims to expand to further games and beyond what we know in our 3D plane of existence...
